About

My 12-year career in the field of visual design has given me a wide range of experience. I started as an exhibitions designer at The Field Museum, where I began designing experiences and interactions that often combined graphic design with other disciplines like multimedia or interaction design.

My experience has taught me to be an excellent brand steward, working within design systems while also creating innovative, user-friendly designs. I leverage my insatiable curiosity, hunger for learning, and drive to create great work. I have a knack for understanding complex problems and finding simple solutions.
