Powerhouse Art Center

Looking to get a taste of working as a freelance designer, I signed up with Catchafire.org to find a good pro bono project. I chose to help the Geneva Powerhouse embark on a major capital campaign to renovate the historic Geneva Car Barn in San Francisco, CA to create a new arts and culture center. I helped by creating a brochure that effectively communicates the opportunity to potential donors. I saved them $3,000

Date: December 2014

Materials: 11 x 14 accordion folds to 11 x 3.5
80# Neenah Royal Birch text
